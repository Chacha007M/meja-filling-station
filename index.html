Our Journey

Meja energy, incorporated as Meja Wholesalers & Retailers, is a petroleum retail and wholesale distribution company founded in January 2021 by its proprietor Mr.Charles Mang’era.

Meja Energy Kenya Limited is committed to providing and distributing quality petroleum products to meet and exceed stakeholders' expectations through outstanding customer service delivery with great regard to the environment.

The right price, quality and quantity of petroleum products are at the heart of the company’s foundational values. This is aimed at increasing consumer trust and promoting brand loyalty.

Meja Energy core business is distribution and sale of petroleum products. The company invests primarily in the retail of petrol and diesel in its petrol stations, we strive to ensure a steady growth by enhancing the competitive edge of existing business and expanding into new areas product lines.

Mission

Our mission is to be a leading local provider and distributor of the right quality & quantity petroleum products to meet and exceed customers and stakeholders expectations through competitive prices to provide the best petroleum product value for consumers in Migori County.

Vision

Our vision is to be the best performing petroleum product retail company in Migori and beyond its borders
